Portugal the Man Appreciation Page

Portugal the Man is unarguably the greatest band that has existed in the history of the universe. Anyone who disagrees is clearly wrong and future generations will look upon these dissenters with great disdain.

About Portugal the Man

Portugal. The Man is an American rock band from Wasilla, Alaska, currently residing in Portland, Oregon. The group consists of lead singer John Baldwin Gourley, Gourley's partner & back-up singer Zoe Manville, Zach Carothers, Kyle O'Quin, Jason Sechrist and Eric Howk.
